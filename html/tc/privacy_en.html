MageNerator.net - Privacy Policy

 

MageNerator.net takes great care to protect your privacy and to secure the data that is stored on the MageNerator.net servers. MageNerator.net employs several security professionals who implement the technical and organizational security during development and operations. MageNerator.net takes measures to protect personal information against loss, misuse, unauthorized access, unauthorized disclosure, manipulation, or destruction.

This Privacy Policy describes how MageNerator.net handles the data it has access to.

Overview

When you use MageNerator.net, you send pieces of data to MageNerator.net; this can be a name, an e-mail address, or information relating to your appointment. Other data is generated implicitly by your use of MageNerator.net, e.g. log data.

MageNerator.net is designed to allow you to use the service almost anonymously if you wish: You can use MageNerator.net without registration and without entering any e-mail address.

Log data

MageNerator.net records certain requests and transactions in log files. This log data is used for troubleshooting, statistics, quality assurance, and to monitor system security and can be analyzed to that end. MageNerator.net can publish anonymous statistics under the condition that no personally identifiable information can be derived from such statistics.

MageNerator.net also uses Piwik to compile usage statistics. Add here more piwik policy

Cookies

When you use MageNerator.net, the service can store cookies on your computer. Cookies are little pieces of information that can help identify your browser and that can store information, e.g. application settings. MageNerator.net uses cookies to track usage, to improve ease-of-use and the overall user experience, and to manage advertising inventories.

Advertising

Advertising is one of MageNerator.net’s revenue streams. Therefore, MageNerator.net displays advertisements on some of the services, e.g. on certain web pages or in e-mails. These advertisements can be targeted based on the information that is available to MageNerator.net, e.g. language, IP address, poll content, etc. This targeting is always an automated process; none of the information used for targeting is analyzed manually or made available to advertising customers. In some cases, MageNerator.net displays advertisements from Google’s AdSense network. These advertisements can be targeted based on the poll content. Google does not disclose that information and only uses it for the intended purpose. You can review Google’s Privacy Policy here: http://www.google.com/privacy.html

Outdated and completed polls

You can delete outdated or completed (or, in fact, any) polls manually at any time using the admin link. When deleting a poll, that poll’s information will be deleted from the MageNerator.net databases. Furthermore, outdated polls are automatically deleted from time to time, but no earlier than 30 days after the last date in the poll, or 30 days after the last access to a poll without dates.

Inactive accounts

MageNerator.net can delete user accounts and Premium MageNerator.net accounts if they remain inactive (i.e. not accessed) for 1 year or longer.

Unauthorized access

MageNerator.net implements several mechanisms to prevent unauthorized access to polls, accounts, or other data.

To access a poll, you use the unique link which MageNerator.net provides and which contains a random access code. This link is an important element to prevent unauthorized access to a poll. You should send this link only to authorized people, i.e., the people you wish to participate in your poll.

MageNerator.net accounts are protected by passwords. You should choose a secure password and ensure its confidentiality to prevent unauthorized access to your account.

Inviting from MageNerator.net

As an option, you can contact and invite people to participate in a poll from MageNerator.net. In this case, MageNerator.net needs to know the e-mail addresses of the people to be contacted. You can enter these e-mail addresses in the corresponding form. MageNerator.net stores these e-mail addresses in your user account (MyMageNerator.net) for your convenience; MageNerator.net will be able to offer you the same contacts again next time you use the service. You can have MageNerator.net delete all stored e-mail addresses any time using the corresponding function in your user account (MyMageNerator.net).

General remark: Like all other personal information, MageNerator.net will not share participants' e-mail addresses with third parties. MageNerator.net will not send any e-mail communication to participants' e-mail addresses without your consent.

Contacts integration

As an alternative (or complement) to the MageNerator.net contacts, you can use your existing Google contacts to gain access to the e-mail addresses of the people to be contacted. In this case, you authorize MageNerator.net to access your Google contacts. Your Google contacts will still be stored at Google, while MageNerator.net makes a copy of the e-mail addresses to have efficient access. Please note also: The process of accessing your Google contacts is set up (thanks to OAuth) to make sure that MageNerator.net at no time has access to your Google password. You can delete the e-mail addresses stored at MageNerator.net at any time (Link -> "Delete all cached contacts").

Plugins

MageNerator.net provides optional plugins which extend the service by integrating it into your local environment (calendar, contacts). A plugin is a software that you download and install on your computer, and which has access to your calendar and contacts.

MageNerator.net will use the via plugins accessible information only for the intended purpose. MageNerator.net will only access the information that is necessary to provide the intended service.

Communication between you and MageNerator.net

If you send MageNerator.net a message, this message can be stored to process it, to compile statistical information, to improve our services and support, or to get in touch with you.

Payment processing

MageNerator.net supports the leading payment processing provider, PayPal. You can find their privacy policies here: PayPal. To make payments as easy and user-friendly as possible, MageNerator.net sends your name and e-mail address to Paypal during a payment process. All this information would be requested by the provider anway.

Location of data

The MageNerator.net databases are located in Switzerland.

Sharing personal information

MageNerator.net uses personal information according to the Terms of Service and this Privacy Policy.

MageNerator.net may share personal information with other companies or individuals only in the following limited circumstances: (i) MageNerator.net has your consent; (ii) MageNerator.net has good faith that there is a legal obligation to share the data; (iii) MageNerator.net needs to access or share the data to protect the security of the service or of others users’ data; (iv) MageNerator.net needs to access or share the data to protect MageNerator.net’s rights and property or to enforce the Terms of Service.

Changes

MageNerator.net reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. The most current version of this policy is available at http://MageNerator.net.com/about/policy.html.
